We deliver scientifically formulated and nutritionally well-balanced shrimp feed to our farmers. Our highly equipped testing labs ensure quality of feed, and our technical teams assist farmers across India with good aquaculture practices, seed selection and seed quality analysis. Not only has this earned us the trust of the country’s farming community but secured a reputable clientele throughout the world.

By introducing competent and innovative technologies, we aim to improve shrimp culture development and feed manufacturing in India. Our emphasis is on productivity, reliability and customer service. By providing our farmers with high quality feed and seed, we obtain better shrimp produce, which is further processed upgraded into export-ready shrimp products for our customers around the world.
